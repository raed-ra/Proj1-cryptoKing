ABOUT CRYPTO KINGS

What is Crypto Kings?

Crypto Kings is a crypto currency trading app that gives you a virtual "bank" containg USD$100,000 in virtual currency to buy and sell crypto currency, using live, real-world crypto currency data, with the aim of generating a profit.

INSTRUCTIONS

Sharpen your crypto trading skills with Crypto Kings.

Watch your virtual money pile up or go bust trying!

1. To view additional data and allocate your funds simply click the holdings value button in the corresponding cryptocurrency row you are interested in.

2. In the popup window, enter the amount of cryptocurrency that you would like to buy or sell and then click either the buy or sell button to execute the trade.

3. Your buying capacity is limited by the funds you have remaining in your "bank" (the game starts with $100,000).

4. Your selling capacity is limited by the holdings you have of the crypto currency you'd like to sell.

5. Your holdings will go up or down each time you refresh the page, according to the change in value of your currencies.
